Production Planning Made Simple

At Entize, we believe that production planning is a cornerstone of every manufacturing company. That is why we built Entize Planning, production planning software designed to make planning as simple and easy as possible.

It doesn't need to be complicated!

Easy to Maintain

Continuous gathering of necessary information makes it effortless to make changes to your production system, such as adding new products or processes.

Per Company, Not Per User

Entize Planning licenses are sold to each company irrespective of the number of users. This helps us break down communication barriers within your organization by sharing actionable information throughout the whole of your organization.

Great Visualization

Production plans can be complicated and difficult to understand. With Entize Planning the plan is beautifully visualized, making it accessible to whomever needs it!

Easy Drag & Drop Planning

Planning is as easy as dragging and dropping an order to when and where it is supposed to be worked on.

How Entize Planning Works

Entize Planning works perfectly with pull production systems, where material and work in progress (WIP) are pulled down the production system by orders placed by the customer. Each step of your production system is planned in a specific view in Entize Planning, where the time horizon and the degree of detail needed are completely customizable.

We continuously grab customer orders from your existing ERP system so you can keep the plan updated in real-time. When an order is planned, an internal order is sent to the previous production step - starting with your customers ordering finished products, all the way back to you ordering the raw materials you need to start working on the product.

Entize Planning works with production systems of all sizes and complexity, from a simple linear system to branched systems with parallel processes.

Seamless integration

Entize Planning integrates perfectly with your existing ERP system, continuously grabbing the information it needs from your databases.

All Information In One Place

Planning becomes even more simple when you have all the information you need in front of you, on one screen.

Cross-platform Design

Entize Planning is completely web-based and cross-platform, meaning that it is accessible to whoever needs it, whenever and wherever.
